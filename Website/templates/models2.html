Models

Non-ML Classifier
Used existing "Positive/Negative" Word Bank
Compares each word in document to database
uses a modified Term Frequency Equation

Naive Bayes Classifer
Used CountTokenize() to split the words and make a vocabulary
Used TF-IDF to assign scores to each word
Used NBClassifier() and Pipeline from SKLearn to build model

SGD Classifier
Same setup as Naive Bayes.
Instead of NBClassifier(), used SGDClassifier()

Neural Network Model
Same initial setup as previous 2 models
Constructed a neural net with 14319 input parameters (one for each word in vocab of training data)
Structure: Dense Layer(243) -> Dropout Layer(.2) -> Dense Layer(27) -> Dropout Layer(.2) -> Dense Layer(3)
After experimentation, best optimizer was ADAGRAD. Also tried ADAM, SGD, ADADELTA, and RMSPROP
After experimentation, 200 Epochs gave us good results. Tried between 20 and 1000.
